3. The SAE Framework

The SAE framework stands for Understanding, Designing, Developing, Interacting.

  • Understanding: Analyzing user needs and cultural differences.
  • Designing: Creating wireframes and architecture.
  • Developing: Coding with APIs and databases.
  • Interacting: Ensuring effective communication via intuitive interfaces.
